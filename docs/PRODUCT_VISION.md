# Product Vision: Rating Microservice

> **Objective Prioritization through Data-Driven Scoring**

## Vision Statement
To provide a transparent, objective, and multi-dimensional scoring framework that eliminates guesswork in the bid/no-bid process, ensuring that the organization always pursues the most strategically aligned and winnable opportunities.

## The Strategy
| Aspect | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Target Group** | Ressort Leads (Björn), Managers (Daniel), and Bid Specialists (Sascha). |
| **Needs** | Multi-criteria scoring, historical benchmark comparison, and customizable rating weights. |
| **Product** | An analytical microservice providing real-time scoring based on enriched tender data and organizational metadata. |
| **Business Goals** | Increase win rate by 15%, reduce "lost bid" overhead by 20%, and achieve 100% scoring transparency. |

## Key Features
1.  **Multi-Criteria Scoring Engine**: Configurable weights for Strategy, Technology, Financials, and Risk.
2.  **Historical Benchmarking**: Comparing new tenders against previous winning/losing profiles.
3.  **Real-Time Prioritization**: Instant recalculation of scores when tender metadata or weights change.
4.  **Explainability**: Detailed breakdown of why a tender received a specific score.

## Success Metrics
- **Rating Accuracy**: Correlation between high scores and successful bid decisions.
- **Resource Efficiency**: Reduction in hours spent evaluating low-rated tenders.
- **Stakeholder Trust**: % of ratings accepted by Decision Makers without manual override.
