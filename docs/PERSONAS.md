# Stakeholder Personas for Rating Service

The **Rating** microservice is the analytical evaluator of the ecosystem. It quantifies the value of every tender.

## 1. [Björn, the Ressort Lead](../../docs/product/personas/bjoern/persona.md)
**Role:** Executive Decision Maker
**Relationship to Rating:**
- **Responsibility:** Ensuring the cluster focuses on high-margin, strategic deals.
- **Needs:** Aggregated "Strategic Fit" scores to prioritize resources across the ressort.
- **Pain Point:** Teams wasting time on low-probability or low-value bids.

## 2. [Daniel, the Manager](../../docs/product/personas/daniel/persona.md)
**Role:** Delivery Manager & Bid Mentor
**Relationship to Rating:**
- **Responsibility:** Balancing customer delivery with new bid success.
- **Needs:** "Technical Feasibility" and "Resource Availability" scores to decide if a team can realistically handle a bid.
- **Pain Point:** Overcommitting teams to complex tenders that result in failed bids or delivery stress.

## 3. [Sascha, the Decision Maker](../../docs/product/personas/sascha/persona.md)
**Role:** Public Sector Expert
**Relationship to Rating:**
- **Responsibility:** Fine-tuning the rating algorithms based on historical win/loss data.
- **Needs:** Transparency in how scores are calculated (e.g., weights for technical requirements vs. price).
- **Pain Point:** "Black box" ratings that don't align with his expert intuition.

## 4. [Julian, the Strategy Analyst](../../docs/product/personas/julian/persona.md)
**Role:** Portfolio Expert
**Relationship to Rating:**
- **Responsibility:** Identifying market shifts based on tender criteria.
- **Needs:** Correlation between high-rated tenders and specific technology labels (e.g., Cloud, AI).
- **Pain Point:** Difficulty in proving that the market is moving towards a specific technology without quantified data.
